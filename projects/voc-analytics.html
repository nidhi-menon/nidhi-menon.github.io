About

Built a platform to automatically classify, cluster, and route incoming support tickets across Autodesk product lines. Combined topic modeling with fine-tuned classifiers to surface trending issues and reduce mean time to resolution. The platform contributed to a 10.7% reduction in support ticket volume across 10+ product teams.

Details

  • Fine-tuned BERT-based classifiers for multi-label ticket categorization across product domains
  • Applied topic modeling to surface emerging issue clusters before they became high-volume
  • Built a real-time routing layer to direct tickets to the right team without manual triage
  • Integrated with existing support tooling via FastAPI microservice
