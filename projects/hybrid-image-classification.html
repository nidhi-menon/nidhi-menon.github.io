Hybrid Image Classification

Combining CNNs and transformers for robust image recognition

About

Georgia Tech graduate research exploring hybrid architectures that fuse convolutional feature extraction with transformer-based attention. The project investigated whether the local feature sensitivity of CNNs and the global context modeling of transformers could be combined to outperform either architecture alone.

Details

  • Benchmarked on CIFAR-10, CIFAR-100, and ImageNet subsets
  • Evaluated accuracy vs. compute tradeoffs across multiple architecture variants
  • Explored early fusion, late fusion, and cross-attention integration strategies
