Floor Layout Optimization via Genetic Algorithms

Evolutionary optimization for architectural space planning

About

Published research applying genetic algorithms to the facility layout problem — optimizing multi-room floor plans under adjacency, area, and circulation constraints. The work demonstrated convergence behavior and evaluated fitness landscape properties across a range of building configurations.

Details

  • Formulated floor plan arrangement as a combinatorial optimization problem with multi-constraint fitness function
  • Applied genetic operators (crossover, mutation, selection) tuned for spatial constraint satisfaction
  • Analyzed convergence behavior and fitness landscape topology across configuration families
