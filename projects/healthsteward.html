HealthSteward

Privacy-first agentic AI health coordination system

About

I started building HealthSteward for myself. Managing endometriosis, PCOS, adenomyosis, and several immune comorbidities means coordinating care across multiple specialists, keeping track of records from different systems, and preparing for appointments without losing the thread of what was said three visits ago. No existing tool handled this in a way that took privacy seriously.

HealthSteward consolidates fragmented medical records across providers and conditions. No health data is transmitted to external services unless explicitly anonymized — all sensitive processing stays local.

Key Features

  • AI-powered visit prep that generates personalized questions for upcoming appointments based on recent records and history
  • Local PDF parsing of after-visit summaries via Ollama with qwen2.5:7b — no cloud required
  • PII anonymization layer for external LLM calls, combining deterministic parsing with targeted LLM calls for unstructured sections
  • Multi-provider record consolidation with condition and medication tracking
